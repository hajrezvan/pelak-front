1# Why Import Products from Iran?

Iran offers a wide range of high-quality products, including saffron, carpets, pistachios, machinery, and more. Due to its strategic location and diverse industries, Iran is an attractive source for global importers.

2# Top Products to Import from Iran

Here are some of the most popular and profitable Iranian products for importers:

  • Saffron: The world’s most valuable spice.
  • Pistachios and Nuts: High quality, large export volumes.
  • Carpets and Handicrafts: Renowned worldwide for craftsmanship.
  • Petrochemical Products: Including base oils and raw materials.
  • Machinery and Industrial Equipment: Reliable manufacturers and exporters.

4# Understanding Import Regulations and Compliance

Iran’s trade environment has some unique regulations. Importers should be aware of sanctions, customs duties, documentation requirements, and legal considerations to ensure smooth transactions.

  • Check for current sanctions
  • Prepare proper invoices and certificates of origin
  • Work with customs brokers familiar with Iran
